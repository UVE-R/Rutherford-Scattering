Click Start to choose between the Simulation and the Derivation. Click on the picture to select either one.

The simulation will open a new window.
Start will cause the simulation to start.
Pause will pause the simulation.
Trail will show the path that the alpha particles take.
To select the foil and mica sheets, you must click pause and click the restart button.
To exit the simulation, click X in the top right.

The derivation contains pictures of how the Rutherford scattering formula was derived.

Sources page has links to websites I have used for information. You can highlight and copy the links.

Quit will close the entire program.
